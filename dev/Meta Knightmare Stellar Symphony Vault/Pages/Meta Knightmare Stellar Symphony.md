## About
A non-profit fast-paced Hack N Slash Action game based on Hal Labs & Nintendo’s ‘Kirby’ Series and the character ‘Meta Knight’.

The artstyle is based on the 'Flagship' titles such as 'Kirby and the Amazing Mirror' & 'Kirby Squeak Squad'.

Created in GameMaker and StarDream Framework.
