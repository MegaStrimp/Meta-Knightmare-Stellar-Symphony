### Story
On a cold night in Dream Land, a mysterious celestial event covers Planet Popstar’s skies with shining stars, constellations, and other celestial anomalies.

Unaware of what is happening, Meta Knight investigates only to discover that various tangible illusions surround him. Creatures from his memories and imaginations begin to appear, coming from star-shaped portals, and eventually knock him down along with his sword Galaxia.

Meta Knight gets back up to take on the celestial enemies, takes back his sword, and finds out that Galaxia has gained a new power; the power to control constellations and harness their unique powers. Accompanied by the stars, Meta Knight will manifest abilities from the depths of his mind, solve the mystery of this celestial event, and defeat the illusions in Celestial Hotspots to halt the invasion in one night.

It is later revealed that the celestial event was caused by Andromeda, the Angel of Stars, to invade Dream Land. For his physical form to appear, Andromeda’s surroundings must be in a stellar delusion state, which comes with a catch. Ones with a strong will can use the stars to their advantage and manifest their desires to reality.

After passing through various stages; Meta Knight reaches the core of the invasion, Ad Astra, where the stellar delusions are at their peak. He will take on the Angel of Stars and stop the invasion.

Throughout the game, Meta Knight will find several ‘Raw Crystals’ as optional collectibles; which are solidified remains of the celestial event, holding little less delusion potential than in Ad Astra. Interacting with the crystals makes Meta Knight dream of encounters from his past and dealing with such encounters will make him stronger.

### Potential Sequel
After defeating Andromeda’s final form, Cosmo Genesis Angel, Meta Knight is exposed to the unfiltered power of Ad Astra and is mesmerized and enhanced by it. Then he gets unknowingly possessed by the Angel’s willpower. Now the Heir of the Stars, Meta Knight has full control over the celestial event and corrupted by its pure power, he refuses to get rid of it. We see a shot of Meta Knight, chained by the constellations, and with stars shining from his eyes, resembling a galaxy.

The sun rises in Dream Land and the inhabitants wake up. The event still goes on and now it’s up to Kirby to find out the one responsible for it.

### Timeline
The game takes place sometime after Kirby Squeak Squad and before Kirby’s Return to Dream Land, hence why the modern Dream Friends are missing in the story.

It is also the first game in the ‘Stellar Timeline’, which is a new branch of Eternity, not connected to Kirby Gamble Galaxy Stories or any of the other fangames.
