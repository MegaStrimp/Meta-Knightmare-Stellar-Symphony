The HUD is clean and simple. There is the Ability Cycle on the bottom left, the player's healthbar right next to it, and the combo counter on the left center.

If the player is attacking an enemy, their own enemy healthbar appears on the bottom right for 3 seconds. The enemy healthbar can have different appearances based on the occasion.

The Ability Cycle shows the player's current ability in the center, and the next scrollable abilities on the left and right sides.

The player's healthbar scales with their max HP.
