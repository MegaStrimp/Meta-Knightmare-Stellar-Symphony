As the player attacks enemies and various environmental hazards, a Combo Counter on the left side of the screen is filled. There are 5 Stellar Levels the player can achieve by gaining combo points (CMP).

==**Ok - Good - Excellent - HARMONIC - META KNIGHTMARE ULTRA DELUXE**==

The amount of CMP earned depends on the player's current attack, the target enemy's hurt state, and whether or not the player is spamming the same attacks (checked via a 5-index array). CMP also decreases by a large amount if the player gets hit.

When the highest possible Stellar Level is reached, Meta Knight enters a 'Superillusion' state. The background gets various transparent effects, ranging from stars to a giant waving note sheet; and Meta Knight himself gets afterimages & sparkles coming out from him. Along with these, a new layer is added to the stage music. These effects continue until the Stellar Level is lost.
